Good to know

Frequently asked questions

How quickly can you install a new boiler?

In most cases we can fit your new boiler the very next day. Once you accept your fixed-price quote, you'll pick a slot that suits you at checkout.

Is the price really fixed?

Yes. The price you're quoted is the price you pay — including the boiler, standard fitting, flue and VAT. If anything unusual comes up, we'll always agree it with you first.

Are your engineers qualified?

Every installation is carried out by fully Gas Safe registered engineers. We're accredited installers for Worcester Bosch, Vaillant, Ideal and more.

Can I spread the cost?

Absolutely. We offer interest-free (0% APR) finance over up to 10 years, subject to status, so you can spread payments from around £18 a month.

What warranty do I get?

Depending on the boiler you choose, you'll get a manufacturer-backed warranty of up to 12 years for total peace of mind.
